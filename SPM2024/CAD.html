Symposium on Solid and Physical Modeling (SPM) 2024

Call for Presentation-only JCAD Papers

Presentation-only Papers

Authors of papers published in the Journal of Computer-Aided Design (JCAD), excluding evolved papers, survey papers, or communication items, are invited to showcase their work at the 2024 Symposium on Solid and Physical Modeling (SPM2024).
To participate, submit your paper as a "Presentation-only paper" without undergoing further review. This approach allows your research to reach a broader audience promptly and encourages more feedback.
Accepted submissions will be presented orally at the conference and listed in the final program, although they will not be included in the proceedings.
For eligibility to present at SPM2024, your paper must have been published in JCAD between March 1, 2023, and March 1, 2024.

Submission guidelines

Only the original paper abstract is required. You should also include the full journal paper and the citation details, plus DOI.
The submission should be done on the EasyChair website.

Important Dates

Paper Submission Deadline: April 30, 2024
Notification of Acceptance: May 14, 2024
Conference: July 8-10, 2024
